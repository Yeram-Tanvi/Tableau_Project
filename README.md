🎯🎯 Insights from Road Accident Dashboard

- Total casualties and accidents can be compared year-on-year using dynamic filters.

- Slight casualties are significantly higher than fatal and serious casualties.

- Cars and motorcycles contribute to the highest number of road accident casualties.

- Most accidents occur during fine weather conditions compared to snow or fog conditions.

- Dry and wet road surfaces record the majority of accidents.

- Single carriageway roads show the highest accident count among all road types.

- Certain locations/districts are identified as accident hotspots through map visualization.

- The dashboard allows dynamic analysis using severity filters like Fatal, Serious, Slight, and All.

- Interactive filters and parameters help users explore accident trends from different perspectives.

- Year-on-year analysis helps identify whether road safety conditions are improving or worsening.
